## Weekly Rides

All rides depart from the club meeting point. Please arrive ten minutes early so we can set off on time.

### Social Ride

**When:** Saturdays, 9:00 AM
**Distance:** 30–50 km
**Pace:** 20–24 km/h average

A relaxed, no-drop ride ideal for newcomers or anyone wanting an easy-paced outing. We usually stop for coffee halfway round.

### Club Ride

**When:** Sundays, 8:30 AM
**Distance:** 60–100 km
**Pace:** 25–28 km/h average

Our main club ride. A steady tempo through the hills and valleys east of Perth. Riders should be comfortable holding pace in a group.

### Fast Group

**When:** Sundays, 8:00 AM
**Distance:** 80–120 km
**Pace:** 30+ km/h average

For experienced riders looking for a challenging ride. Chain-gang technique is used on open roads. Good bunch-riding etiquette is expected.

## What to Bring

- A roadworthy bike in good condition
- Helmet (mandatory)
- Spare inner tube, pump, and tyre levers
- Water and food for the distance
- Appropriate clothing for the weather
