## Who We Are

Midland Cycling Club is a friendly, inclusive cycling club based in Midland, Perth, Western Australia. We welcome riders of all ages and abilities — from complete beginners to experienced racers.

## Our Rides

We organise regular group rides at various paces so there's always something to suit you. All our rides start from the same meeting point, and ride leaders are on hand to make sure nobody gets left behind on the social groups.

## Club History

Founded by a small group of local cyclists who wanted a relaxed, welcoming club that focused on the simple enjoyment of riding bikes. We've grown steadily over the years but our ethos remains the same: get out, ride together, and have fun.

## Affiliated

We are affiliated with AusCycling, giving our members access to a wide range of competitive events should they wish to race.
