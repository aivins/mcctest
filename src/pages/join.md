## Membership

Joining Midland Cycling Club is straightforward. You're welcome to come along to a couple of rides as a guest before committing.

### What You Get

- Access to all club rides and events
- Club kit at discounted prices
- Insurance coverage through AusCycling affiliation
- A friendly community of like-minded riders
- Invitations to club socials and awards evenings

### How to Join

1. Come along to one of our weekend rides — just turn up at the meeting point
2. If you enjoy it, speak to a ride leader or committee member about joining
3. Complete the membership form and pay your annual subscription

### Membership Fees

- **Adult:** $50 per year
- **Junior (under 18):** $20 per year
- **Family (2 adults + up to 3 juniors):** $100 per year

Membership runs from 1 January to 31 December. If you join after 1 September, your membership carries through to the end of the following year.

For any questions about membership, [get in touch](/contact/).
