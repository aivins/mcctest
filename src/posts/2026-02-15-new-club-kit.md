We're pleased to announce that the **2026 Midland Cycling Club kit** is now available to order.

## What's New

This year's design keeps the classic club colours but with a refreshed layout. The kit is manufactured by a new supplier, which means improved fabrics and a better fit across the range.

### Available Items

- Short-sleeve jersey
- Long-sleeve jersey
- Bib shorts
- Winter jacket
- Gilet

## How to Order

Head to the club kit page on our supplier's website (link will be emailed to all members). Orders close on **28 February**, with delivery expected in April.

If you have any questions about sizing, speak to a committee member at the next club ride.
