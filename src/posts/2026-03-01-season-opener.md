Spring is in the air and it's time to blow out the winter cobwebs! Our annual season opener ride takes place on **Sunday 15 March**, departing from the usual meeting point at 9:00 AM.

## Route

We'll head out through the Swan Valley, loop round through the hills towards Mundaring, and finish up at the café for well-earned coffee and cake. Total distance is around 70 km with a few gentle climbs.

## All Welcome

This ride is open to all groups — we'll split into pace groups at the start, so whether you've been on the turbo all winter or haven't touched the bike since October, come along!

Make sure your bike is in good working order and bring spares. See you there.
