Mark your calendars — the **Midland Cycling Club Annual Awards Dinner** takes place on **Saturday 7 February** at The Railway Hotel.

## The Evening

- 7:00 PM — Arrival and drinks
- 7:45 PM — Dinner
- 9:00 PM — Awards presentation
- 9:30 PM onwards — Social

## Awards

We'll be handing out awards for the past season, including:

- Rider of the Year
- Most Improved Rider
- Volunteer of the Year
- Clubman/Clubwoman of the Year

## Tickets

Tickets are $40 per person, which includes a three-course meal. Partners and guests are welcome. To book, contact the social secretary or sign up at the next club ride.
